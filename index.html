Shane Levac (1981 - 2020)

Gone but NEVER forgotten

Who was Shane Levac?

A husband, father, son, brother, friend and a versatile musician.

On October 17th, 2020, we all lost one of the most remarkable human beings we had the privilege to call a friend.

Shane Andrew Levac was known for the music he created, the connections he made with his kind soul. If you knew Shane you would always be greeted with the tightest hug and a “how’s it going”. Shane had a special way to brighten up any room with that laugh and intense sense of humour.

Kylie and Shane made an incredible couple! Kylie was definitely the love of his life. She grounded him and they created a wonderful life. 4½ months ago they welcomed their son Oliver into this world. Shane loved his family with his whole heart, they were his everything.

Lets keep his memory alive so little Oliver can know he had a phenomenal daddy.

We love you Shane!

Gone but never forgotten.
